Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

🔒 Your privacy matters to us. Pulse of Mzansi is committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

1. Who We Are

Pulse of Mzansi is a South African real-time opinion and voting platform operated by Paul Nel. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at pulseofmzansi@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you register and use the app, we collect the following information:

  • Email address and password (for authentication)
  • Phone number (optional, provided at sign up)
  • Province, age, gender and race (for demographic insights)
  • Your votes and voting history
  • Income, education, employment and other optional profile data
  • City or town of residence (optional)
  • Political alignment and social media usage (optional)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account
  • Record and display your votes
  • Generate anonymised demographic breakdowns of voting results
  • Improve the app and its features
  • Respond to contact requests or question submissions you send us

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Voting results and demographic breakdowns shared publicly or with researchers are always fully anonymised — your individual responses are never identified or shared.

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication and data storage
  • EmailJS — for processing contact form submissions
  • Expo / EAS — for app distribution

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access.

Your password is never stored in plain text — Firebase handles authentication securely using industry best practices.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at pulseofmzansi@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Pulse of Mzansi is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app. Your continued use of the app after changes are made constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
